How to Use Clothing Forecast

It's super simple!

First, you make your own account, which will give you your personalized suggestions.


You'll need to put in your location, but don't worry, this can be changed along with your password later! Once you are done, you will need to log in to access your account.

When you log in, you will be greeted by the dashboard with your location, weather, and temperature.


You will also see our suggested choices for the current weather. This will change to be more personalized as you add more data to it.


Whenever you access your dashboard, it will take some time to load. Don't panic! The machine is working hard to produce your results.


On the top, you will see the navigation bar. Explore the options! They will become available once you log in.

On the dashboard, you will see the options recommended to you.


To get more accurate results, think about the options and select the ones you dislike and press the button at the bottom to train the machine.


If you do not see an option that you like, you can click the 'Explore more options' button to add them.


You can either see our recommended suggestions if your selection becomes too narrow, or you can put a specific combination in yourself.

Important note: This machine is loosely trained on intervals of temperature and weather conditions and may not immediately be accurate.


To make it more accurate, please give it your input.


Also, changes may not happen immediately, it may take a couple of times for it to fully register and remember.


You may notice sometimes that the machine will recommend a wide range. This is normal when the machine is indecisive. You can make it more accurate by whittling down the options, making it more specific.
